gozora commented at 2018-03-22 17:58:¶

Hi,

Try to add OUTPUT_OPTIONS="rw" to your local.conf
If it does not help, attach relevant log file from rear mkbackup session to this issue.

Be aware, that USB_DEVICE device can't be mounted during rear mkbackup/mkrescue.run time.

V.
