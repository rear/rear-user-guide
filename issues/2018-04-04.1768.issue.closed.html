gozora commented at 2018-04-04 10:34:¶

You are doing backup to local directory (BACKUP_URL="file:///rearbackup").
In order to restore from such configuration, you need to have your backup archive (backup.tar.gz) available in /rearbackup/ch13x777 directory prior running rear recover.
