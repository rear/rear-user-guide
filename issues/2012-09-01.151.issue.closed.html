UUID=28f657a5-5242-46df-9fd4-87060cd43aa2 / ext4 errors=remount-ro 0 1
in /etc/fstab mount produces since Debian wheezy an output of
/dev/disk/by-uuid/28f657a5-5242-46df-9fd4-87060cd43aa2 on / type ext4 (rw,relatime,errors=remount-ro,user_xattr,barrier=1,data=ordered)
